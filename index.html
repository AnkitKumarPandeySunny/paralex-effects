Adventure Time

Adventure Time is an American animated television series created by Pendleton Ward for Cartoon Network in 2010. The show is about a speaking dog with shape-shifting powers named Jake and his best friend Finn the Human who embark on many bizarre adventures in the post-apocalyptic Land of Ooo (Zahed).
